Buns

Description

Deliciuosly made Buns delicacies for successful men!

Our Buns will keep you coming back for more

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • Sugar
  • Vegetable Oils
  • Cup Minced Onions

Steps

  1. Spread a thin layer of flour in the bottom of a baking dish
  2. Make a layer of cooked bun noodles
  3. Spread an even layer of the veg mixture.
  4. Spread an even layer of meat sauce.
  5. Repeat those layers two times.
  6. Top it with a final layer of noodles, sauce, mozzarella, and parmesan cheese to the bun
